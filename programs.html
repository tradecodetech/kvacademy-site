KV Academy Programs

Youth Lawn Care Entrepreneurship

A responsibility and character-building program where students learn teamwork, time management, work ethic, accountability, and the basics of service-based entrepreneurship in a supervised setting.

STEM & Coding Lab

Students explore computer science fundamentals, website creation, digital design, automation basics, and hands-on STEM projects. The lab encourages creativity, innovation, and problem-solving in a supportive environment suitable for all skill levels.

Leadership & Literacy Development

A youth development track designed to strengthen communication, reading comprehension, public speaking, confidence, and constructive decision-making. Students participate in activities that promote character, teamwork, emotional intelligence, and community awareness.
